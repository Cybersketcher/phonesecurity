Cyber Crimes Happening Through Mobile Phones

Types of Cyber Crimes on Phone

  • Phishing Calls & Messages
  • SIM Card Swapping
  • WhatsApp Account Hijacking
  • OTP Fraud & Banking Scam
  • Malicious Apps Installation
  • Fake Customer Care Scams
  • Screen Sharing Scams
  • Ransomware & Spyware Attacks

How to Stay Safe & Prevent Cyber Crimes

  • Never share OTP, PIN, passwords with anyone.
  • Use strong screen lock & enable 2-factor authentication.
  • Avoid downloading unknown apps or APK files.
  • Do not click unknown links received via SMS or WhatsApp.
  • Verify customer care numbers from official websites only.
  • Block/report suspicious numbers immediately.
  • Use antivirus & keep your phone updated.
  • Disable screen sharing unless absolutely trusted.
