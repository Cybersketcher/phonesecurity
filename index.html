Cyber Crimes Happening Through Mobile Phones

Types of Cyber Crimes

  • Phishing Calls & Messages
  • SIM Card Swapping
  • WhatsApp Account Hijacking
  • OTP & Banking Fraud
  • Malicious Apps & Spyware
  • Fake Customer Care Scams
  • Screen Sharing Scams
  • Ransomware Attacks

How to Stay Safe

  • Never share OTP, PIN, passwords.
  • Enable 2-step verification on all apps.
  • Avoid unknown apps & APK installs.
  • Don’t click unknown links.
  • Verify customer care numbers yourself.
  • Use antivirus & phone updates.
  • Disable screen sharing with strangers.
  • Report suspicious numbers immediately.
  • Report cyber fraud immediately at 1930.
  • File complaints at cybercrime.gov.in.
